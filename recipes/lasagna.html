World's Best Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Two Cloves of Garlic
  • Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • Two Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • Two Cans of Tamato Paste
  • Two Tablespoons of Sugar
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Ricotta Cheese
  • An Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna accordingly:
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta Mixture
    4. Mozzarella Slices
    5. Meat Sauce
    6. Parmesan Cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
