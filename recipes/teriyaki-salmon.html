Teriyaki Salmon

Description

A rice bowl with salmon marinated in teriyaki, edamame, and chili oil.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • White Rice
  • Edamame
  • Teriyaki Marinade
  • Chili Oil
  • Roasted Sesame Seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Marinate salmon in teriyaki for at least one hour.
  2. Cook rice.
  3. Defrost/cook edamame.
  4. Cut salmon into equal chunks. Cook in air fryer for 8 minutes at 350Â° F.
  5. Add rice, edamame, and salmon to a bowl and top with a spoonful of chili oil and sesame seeds.
