Overnight Oats

Description

A quick and easy breakfast that can be prepared the night before.

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder (15g)
  • Plain Fat Free Greek Yogurt (150g)
  • Oats (60g)
  • Almond Milk (125ml)
  • Peanut Butter (15g)
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Add oats, greek yogurt, and protein powder to mason jar (or any container).
  2. Add almond milk and mix.
  3. Store in the fridge overnight.
  4. Top with peanut butter and banana before consuming.
