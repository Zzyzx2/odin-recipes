Stir Fry

Description

A quick and easy stiry fry dish from Gordon Ramsay.

Ingredients

  • Rice Noodles
  • Chicken Breast
  • Garlic
  • Broccoli
  • Olive Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Egg
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Soak rice noodles in hot water for 12-15 minutes.
  2. Heat wok.
  3. Cut chicken breast into thin strips.
  4. Finely slice the garlic.
  5. Slice broccoli into bite size pieces.
  6. Add olive oil to the wok.
  7. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Continuously stir chicken. When it begins to sear, add garlic.
  9. Add broccoli.
  10. Add soy sauce.
  11. Remove contents from the wok, add another teaspoon of olive oil.
  12. Drain the noodles.
  13. Whisk eggs in a bowl, then continue to whisk in the pan.
  14. Add noodles and cooked chicken, broccoli, garlic.
  15. Squeeze fresh lime over the dish.
